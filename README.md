## About The Project
**UCSF Tuberculosis Human Dose Prediction Tool** developed by the Savic Lab in support of the **Multi-model Landscape to Integrate Machine Learning, Site of Action, and Mechanistic PKPD Translational Tools for Improved Selection of TB Drug Combination Regimens and Informed Dose Selection** project funded by the Gates Foundation. This tool predicts clinical efficacious dose ranges for anti-TB compounds in development based on *in vitro* and early *in vivo* PK and PD data.
